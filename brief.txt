Jan Kosutnik — personal site brief
===================================

One page. Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS. No framework. No build step.
Font: iA Writer Quattro (jsDelivr) + IBM Plex Mono for labels.
Colors: warm near-white #f6f5f1, near-black #1c1b18.
Width: wide, generous whitespace, ia.net level of calm.
Light mode only.

The interaction: telescopic text (Ted Nelson StretchText).
Dotted underline words expand inline on click. Can nest.
Each expands once. Keyboard accessible.
"Unfold everything" / "Collapse" controls.
