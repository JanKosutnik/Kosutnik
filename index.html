Work

I build small systems that stay out of the way.

Most problems are not technical first - they're decision problems. I start with the decision, not the stack, and build the smallest thing that solves it. Sometimes that means AI. Sometimes it's deleting a tool, changing a process, or talking you out of the impressive version.

I can follow a decision from the first sketch to the last pixel to the test that keeps it honest.

Principles

01Before you build something, ask whether it needs to exist.
02If you can remove it and nothing breaks, remove it.
03A good tool stays out of your way. The best one, you forget you are using.
04Every feature you add is a promise to keep it working.
