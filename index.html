Evergreenprinciples I keep returning to

  • 02If you can remove it and nothing breaks, remove it.
  • 03A good tool stays out of your way. The best one, you forget you're using.
  • 04Every feature you add is a promise to keep it working - forever.
  • 05Most "must-have" features are just habits no one ever questioned.
  • 06When something feels confusing, it's not you. It's the design.
  • 07Simple isn't less. It's everything that matters and nothing that doesn't.
